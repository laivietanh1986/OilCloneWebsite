About Us

We Are Thai Vegetable Oil

Thai vegetable oil public company limited was established since  1985, the Company registered in Stock Exchange of Thailand in 1990. TVO is Southeast Asia's largest soybean oil processor, governing four crushing facilities and three refineries in Thailand. The company utilizes soybean over 2,000,000 tons annually which can convert to cooking oil up to 400,000 tons per year. The company has production capacity 6,000 ton of soybean per day, manufacture and distribute products both domestic and global market. The products category are meal and edible oil.

TVO commits to produce quality products to deliver quality of life to consumers through products which leads to vision of being an Asian leading vegetable oil manufacturer. The company will conduct a business to be aligned with corporate government principle with responsibility to develop social and environment.
