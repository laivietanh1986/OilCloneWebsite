About Us

We Are Milton Vegetable Oil

1. Who we are ?

Milton vegetable oil public company limited was established since  1985,Registered in Stock Exchange of Miltonland in 1990. Largest soybean oil processor in Southeast Asia. Our main product comprised of meal and edible oil.

2. Quality

Milton’s passion for quality is evident in the taste. All Milton products are constantly tested, improved, guaranteeing that only the best ends up on your table!

3. Our promise

Our products are dedicated for the well-being of people. Our production policy corresponds with social and environmental concern. Being aware of the status of the environment, Milton has taken multiple initiatives to ensure the production of our products is sustainable.

4. Our Vision

Our company strives to grow and develop towards one ultimate idea: to create a sustainable living among people. TVO commits to quality: we strive to cement the position of the leading manufacture of vegetable oil in Southeast Asia and expand the horizon by becoming one of largest vegetable oil manufacture in all Asia.

5. Fact

Production capacity: 6000 ton of soybean/ day. Annually: 2,000,000 ton which can convert into 400,000 ton of cooking oil.
